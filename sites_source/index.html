More Than Just A Bridal Shop

Elegant · Sophisticated · Class

For more than twenty years we have welcomed brides from right across Cheshire — from the cobbled streets of Chester to the villages of the Cheshire plain — and helped each one uncover the gown that feels unmistakably hers.

There is no crush, no queue and no clock ticking. During your appointment the boutique belongs to you alone, so you can relax, take your time, and enjoy every moment of choosing the dress you will remember for the rest of your life.
