Finding “The One”, The Lovely Way

Dress shopping should be one of the highlights of planning your wedding, not something you brace yourself for. We've spent years fine-tuning a calm, unhurried appointment so all you have to do is enjoy the moment your dress appears.

A Few Friendly Tips

Make The Most Of Your Visit

  • Bring one or two people whose opinion you truly trust — too many voices can muddle things.
  • Wear nude, seamless underwear and pop any heels you're considering in your bag.
  • Stay open-minded — the gown you fall for is very often not the one you expected.
  • Give yourself plenty of time so the whole thing stays relaxed and fun.
