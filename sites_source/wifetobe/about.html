Two Decades, Countless Brides

More Than Just A Bridal Shop

Wife To Be began with a simple belief: that choosing a wedding dress should feel special from the very first hello. Over twenty years on, we still greet every bride the same way — warmly, patiently and with all the time in the world to get it right. Brides come to us from Chester, across Cheshire and further afield still, and many arrive on the recommendation of a friend, a sister or a mum we dressed years before.

We keep our appointments private and unhurried on purpose. When it is just you, your people and your stylist, the whole thing feels less like shopping and more like the lovely occasion it should be.
