About Us

Booking software, made with a boutique's eye.

Ivory Digital is a Manchester-based studio dedicated to one thing: giving bridal boutiques beautiful, dependable booking software they actually own.

Bridal is a business of first impressions and precious moments. Yet so many boutiques still juggle a paper diary, missed calls during fittings, and clunky third-party booking widgets that look nothing like their brand — and quietly charge a fee on every booking.

We built Ivory Digital to change that. We create a private, elegant booking system for each boutique: a customer-facing website that feels like a natural extension of your shop, paired with a powerful behind-the-scenes diary that keeps your team calm and in control. No generic widget, no per-booking commission — just software that works the way you do.

How we work

Every system is bespoke. We learn how your boutique runs — your appointment types, your questions, your hours and how many brides you can see at once — and shape the software around it. Then we brand it to you, host it on your domain, and look after it with a simple maintenance package so you can focus on your brides.
