Common Questions

Client gallery software FAQs

What is client gallery software, and how does StudioApp help photographers?

Client gallery software is where you upload, present and deliver a shoot to your clients online. StudioApp gives wedding and portrait photographers beautiful branded galleries, smooth video streaming, guest uploads, automated emails and print sales — all from one dashboard.

How do I deliver wedding photos and video to my couples?

You upload your images and films into a branded gallery, then share a single link (or QR code). Your couple can view, favourite, download and share their photos and watch their film stream smoothly on any device.

Can wedding guests upload their own photos?

Yes. Turn on guest uploads and friends and family can add their own photos and videos straight into the gallery, so every moment from the day ends up in one place.

How much does StudioApp cost?

Plans start at £15 a month for Starter, £35 for Professional and £65 for Studio. Every plan includes unlimited storage, custom branding, guest uploads and activity tracking.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — there's a free 7-day trial with no credit card required, so you can set up your studio and create your first gallery before you commit.
