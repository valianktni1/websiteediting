## Product 1 — Bridal Boutique Booking System
A complete appointment system for bridal boutiques with two sides:

### 1. The customer booking website (public)
A calm, elegant, mobile-friendly booking journey styled to match the boutique's brand:
- Choose a boutique (supports multiple locations, each with its own photo, address, phone and opening hours).
- Choose an appointment type (e.g. bridal appointment, first-look consultation, fitting, gown collection) — each with its own duration (30/60/90/120 minutes) and description.
- Pick a date on an elegant calendar; closed days and holidays are automatically disabled.
- Pick from only the times that are genuinely free.
- Enter details and answer custom questions the boutique defines (e.g. wedding date, dress size, budget, how did you hear about us).
- Receive an instant on-screen confirmation with a unique reference, plus one-tap 'Add to Google Calendar' and a downloadable .ics file.
- Self-serve later: view, reschedule or cancel using the booking reference — no phone call needed.
- If a day is fully booked, join a waitlist instead of leaving.

### 2. The staff control panel (secure, private)
- Dashboard: today's appointments and live counts (upcoming, pending, confirmed, all-time) with a per-boutique breakdown.
- Bookings diary: every booking in one place; filter by boutique or status; open any booking to view full details and the customer's answers; confirm, complete, cancel, mark no-show, add private notes, reschedule (availability is re-checked), or add a follow-up; export all bookings to CSV.
- Customers: a record of everyone who has booked, with contact details and history.
- Analytics: busiest days and times, per-boutique performance, no-show count and rate, and where brides come from (Instagram, Google, word of mouth, etc.).
- Availability: weekly opening hours per boutique; slot interval; capacity (how many appointments at once, e.g. two fitting rooms); buffer between appointments; block whole days (holidays) or part days (e.g. lunch).
- Appointment types: create, edit, hide or remove services and set their length.
- Customise: add each boutique's photo, opening-hours text, intro blurb and a 'what to expect' note; build custom booking questions (short text, long text, date, dropdown; optional or required).
- Waitlist: see everyone waiting and mark them contacted.
- Staff accounts: an owner (superadmin) plus up to four staff logins, each with their own password and optional two-factor authentication; the owner account can never be accidentally locked out.
- Settings & notifications: optional automatic emails (confirmation on request, confirmation when approved, and a reminder 24 hours before) — all off until switched on, and sent from the boutique's own email; plus an iCal subscription URL for Google/Apple Calendar.

## Technology & security
- Built with React, FastAPI and MongoDB.
- Passwords stored hashed (bcrypt); secure token-based (JWT) logins; optional TOTP two-factor authentication.
- A smart booking engine prevents double-booking and only ever offers genuinely free times, respecting hours, capacity, buffers and blocks.

## Product 2 — StudioApp (client galleries for wedding photographers)
StudioApp is the complete client gallery and photo delivery platform for wedding photographers, built by a wedding photographer after delivering over 1,600 weddings. It runs as a web app at https://studioappgallery.uk/ with a free 7-day trial (no credit card required, cancel anytime).

### What StudioApp does
- Branding: upload your studio logo, set your name, tagline, contact email and accent colour; branding appears across galleries, client pages and emails, with branded share links (e.g. yourdomain/s/your-studio/couple-name).
- Galleries: create unlimited client galleries (within your plan), organise into folders/sub-galleries, save reusable folder templates, set cover images and attach a client email to each gallery.
- Uploads: drop 1,000+ photos at once with smart batched uploading, live progress, high-resolution images and video, automatic thumbnails, and copy/move between folders.
- Video: upload films straight into a gallery; automatic web-optimised, hardware-accelerated versions stream smoothly on any device.
- Sharing: secure branded share links with optional passwords and expiry dates; control view-only vs full download; custom memorable link names; instant QR codes and downloadable QR PDFs; optional guest-upload mode.
- Client emails & automation: connect your own sending email (SMTP); send 'your gallery is ready' notifications; reusable templates; broadcast to all clients; automatic expiry reminders 7 days before a gallery closes.
- Print sales: clients order prints directly from their gallery; you receive and manage orders.
- Insights & control: activity tracking, plan usage meter (galleries used vs allowance), full delete control, and everything kept private per studio.

### Compatible file formats
- Photos: JPEG, PNG, GIF, TIFF, plus WebP and BMP.
- Videos: MP4, MOV, plus AVI, MKV, WebM and MTS.

### StudioApp pricing (per month, GBP; all plans include a free 7-day trial)

- Professional — £35/month: 30 active galleries, unlimited storage, video uploads up to 50GB per file, custom branding, guest uploads, activity tracking.
- Studio — £65/month: 60 active galleries, unlimited storage, video uploads up to 50GB per file, custom branding, guest uploads, activity tracking.
- All plans include free SSL, backups and regular updates. Start free at https://studioappgallery.uk/.
